Problem ID: 3834_ToT_(omnimath)
Question: $100$ numbers $1$, $1/2$, $1/3$, $...$, $1/100$ are written on the blackboard. One may delete two arbitrary numbers $a$ and $b$ among them and replace them by the number $a + b + ab$. After $99$ such operations only one number is left. What is this final number?

(D. Fomin, Leningrad)
